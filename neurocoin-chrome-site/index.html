Neurocoin

Neurocoin is a productivity coin that intends to leverage the power of a suite of applications across your desktop, browser, phone, and tablets to track your productivity and reward you based on your efforts. The more productive you are, the more you earn. Neurocoin hopes to eventually expose every user to discounted “brain-boosting” services such as books, academic material, podcasts, wellness subscriptions, e-books, and much more. These discounts will be redeemable with Neurocoin.

How the Chrome Extension Works

Neurocoin for Chrome uses AI to determine if you’re on a productive website or not. A student may commonly use Bruin Learn and will observe an appreciation in the number of coins they receive as they spend more time on the website. Simply put, time is proportional to Neurocoin. When a student switches to a site such as Netflix, they will see that they stop accumulating Neurocoin. Long inactivity is also tracked for each website and that also stops earning Neurocoin.

Algorithm

Neurocoin uses fine-tuned AI to determine productivity. While there may be instances that are a grey area, we correctly identify most productive and unproductive sites.

Modes

Neurocoin for Chrome has two modes: Grind and Normal. Grind mode will notify you when you're being unproductive, which can help you earn more Neurocoin. Normal mode will not notify you when you are unproductive, and is a better mode for people who would like it to run in the background.

Testing Guidelines

Congratualtions on being a tester for Neurocoin, hopefully you'll be a part of something huge. These are the steps you should be following

Setup Steps

  1. Download the zip file and unpack it on your file explorer/finder by double clicking the zip file.
  2. Check if your Chrome version needs an update. If no, proceed. If yes, update it and proceed (will take a minute).
  3. Visit chrome://extensions on Google Chrome.
  4. Check your top right corner. Your developer mode should be toggled on. If developer mode is turned off, turn it on.
  5. Now click "Load unpacked" in the top left corner and navigate to the folder "neurocoin-chrome".
  6. Neurocoin for Chrome should popup under "All extensions". Now click the puzzle icon in the top right corner and pin the extension so you can see it at all times.
  7. Your Neurocoin Extension should now be up and running!

Things to Think About

Here are some things to keep in mind while testing:

  1. Do you feel like accumulating Neurocoin?
  2. How useful is grindmode?
  3. Do you think your Neurocoin count is reflective of your productivity? Remember one coin is one minute of work.
